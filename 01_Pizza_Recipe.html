Ingredients

  • 1 kg plain flour
  • 1.5 tablespoons dry yeast
  • Pinch of sugar
  • 1 heaped tablespoon salt
  • 700ml warm water
  • Oil for greasing

Instructions

  1. Mix yeast, water and sugar and let it sit for 10 minutes.
  2. Then add the salt.
  3. Slowly add the sifted flour and mix
  4. Mix until you get a soft consistency. Add a little oil.
  5. Cover with a pile of sweet cloth and let it rest for at least 30 minutes/1 hour!
